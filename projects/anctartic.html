ANCTARTIC RESOLUTION VIZ

Antarctic Resolution is an ambitious multidisciplinary publication that brings together experts from science, architecture, and the arts to provide an unprecedented, holistic view of Antarctica. The project, led by Giulia Foscari/UNLESS, sheds light on the environmental, geopolitical, and cultural significance of the southernmost continent, combining essays, data, and visuals to create a unique narrative.

As part of the design team, I contributed to the creation of infographics for this publication. My role focused on understanding and organizing the complex data provided, translating it into visual elements under the guidance of the main design team. These infographics complement the content of the book, helping to communicate the crucial issues surrounding Antarctica in an accessible and compelling way.
